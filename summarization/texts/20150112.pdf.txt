REPORT FROM THE SUB - COMMITTEE ON MINUTES

Councilmember Varner made a motion that the minutes of the December 8th,  2015 meeting of
the Council be accepted and placed on file.  Councilmember White seconded the motion which
carried by a voice vote of nine ( 9)  ayes. 

SPECIAL BUSINESS

Council President Scott announced that the South Bend Common Council is accepting
applications for citizen members for its 2015 Standing Committees.  He also announced that
there are openings for the Diversity Utilization Board; Redevelopment Commission (2) 
appointments and Urban Enterprise Association ( 2)  appointments.  He advised that applications
may be found on -line on the Council' s website and in the Office of the City Clerk, 4th Floor
County -City Building, 227 W. Jefferson Blvd., South Bend, Indiana. Applications are to be filed
in the Clerk' s Office by January 20th, 2015. 

REPORTS FROM CITY OFFICES
Tim Scott, President, South Bend Common Council, 4thth Floor County -City Building, 227 W. 
Jefferson Blvd., South Bend,  Indiana, states reports from City offices will be due the fourth
Monday of each month instead of every Council meeting. Mayor Pete and Chief of staff Kathryn
Roos were in attendance.

Attorney on the case not Kathy Cekanski-  Farrand. 

Councilmember Henry Davis reiterated that he wants to move forward with dates for Health and
Public Safety Committee regarding a resolution passed last year in regards to minority

recruitment.

Council can do.
